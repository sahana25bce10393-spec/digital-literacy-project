# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

##  Do’s
- Maintain a professional and respectful profile
- Share useful and positive content
- Verify information before posting
- Use privacy settings to protect your data
- Think before posting anything online

##  Don’ts
- Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly
- Don’t post offensive or harmful content
- Don’t spread fake news or rumors
- Don’t engage in unnecessary arguments online
- Don’t waste excessive time on social media
