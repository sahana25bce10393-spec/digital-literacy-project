# Prevention Checklist: Keep Yourself Safe Online#  General Safety AdviceMake sure every account has a strong and distinctive password.
Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA).
Steer clear of clicking on dubious or unknown links.
Check messages and emails before replying.
Avoid disclosing private information on open platforms.
When browsing, make use of secure websites (HTTPS).
Update your devices and apps on a regular basis.
When downloading files from unidentified sources, exercise caution.#  Financial Security and UPI
Never divulge your bank information, OTP, or UPI PIN to third parties.
Prior to authorizing transactions, always confirm payment requests.#  Report Cybercrime
https://cybercrime.gov.in is the National Cybercrime Portal.
